LC Pycnonotus goiavier

Posted on: 17 Sep, 2022 PST
Categories: Animals Birding
Tags: Pycnonotus goiavier yellow-vented bulbul luklak

  • English: Yellow-vented Bulbul
  • Filipino: Luklak

This is among my very first bird videos.

They are residents here in my area, possibly numbering more than a dozen. I hear them around 07:00 to 09:00, hanging out together with the matang dulong.

As per Wikipedia, there are six recognised subspecies. Based on my location, I believe my companions are of the nominate subspecies P. g. goiavier, found in northern and north-central Philippines.
