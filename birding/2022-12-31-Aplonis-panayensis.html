LC Aplonis panayensis

Posted on: 31 Dec 2022 20:30:00 PST
Categories: Animals Birding
Tags: Aplonis panayensis asian glossy starling galansiyang

  • English: Asian glossy starling
  • Filipino: Galansiyang

This is among my very first bird videos.

They are residents here in my area, usually spotted very early in the morning. They like to hang out on the electricity lines.
